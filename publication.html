Publications I completed my Ph.D. from Symbiosis International University, India, on Automated Lexical Simplification of Hindi Text. The objective of the research was to improve the accessibility of text in Hindi by automating the identification of complex, that is, difficult to understand words, and providing simpler alternatives to the target complex words. A part of this research was sponsored by Symbiosis Centre for Research and Innovation, Symbiosis International University, and approved by the Independent Ethics Committee, Symbiosis International University. The resources created individually, as well as in collaboration with others are as follows:
  • CWID-hi Dataset
  • Hindi Stop Word List
  • Hindi Aesthetics Corpus
  • ACL Anthology Browser
  • Code for Ablation Study
The code and data for the studies can be found at:
  • Complex Word Identification
  • Bibliometric Analysis of Studies on Lexical Simplification
