Moodle LMS

  • I am well versed with Moodle LMS for teaching, conducting summative and formative assessments, as well as for administration. I have developed custom Moodle plugins for my college using the Moodle API, and have contributed to MoodleNet, a social network for educators, as an alpha tester.
  • Conducted Moodle LMS training for university faculty and system administrators. I have also given a talk on Moodle at gnuNify, an international open source event held at Symbiosis Institute of Computer Studies and Research.
  • Created a user manual for Moodle LMS for university faculty members and system administrators.

    Word Embeddings
    Word embeddings are vector representations of words formed by training a model on huge amounts of textual data. I delivered a talk on word embeddings at a faculty development workshop organized by Symbiosis Institute of Computer Studies and Research.
  • Women Who Code Connect Forward
    Lexical Simplification of Hindi text
    Lexical simplification refers to the process of simplifying complex words in a given text using machine learning techniques. I spoke on this topic at Women Who Code Connect Forward 2020.
